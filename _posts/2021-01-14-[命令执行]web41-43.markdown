### Web41

```php
if(isset($_POST['c'])){
  $c = $_POST['c'];
if(!preg_match('/[0-9]|[a-z]|\^|\+|\~|\$|\[|\]|\{|\}|\&|\-/i', $c)){
    eval("echo($c);");
  }
}else{
  highlight_file(__FILE__);
}
?>
```
